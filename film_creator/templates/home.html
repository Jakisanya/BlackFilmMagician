PURPOSE

Our purpose is to enable the film industry to increase its Black film production - films with a majority-Black lead/supporting cast. The power of AI will provide the data to support film ideas at all stages through plot development and cast curation. Our goal is to inspire and support filmmakers - to dramatically increase the chance that a Black film secures funding.
